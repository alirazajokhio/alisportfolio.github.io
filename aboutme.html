My Story

Hi, I am Ali Raza Asghar Jokhio. A Criminologist turned data analyst with 15+ projects completed in SQL, Python, Power BI, and Tableau. Proven ability to collect, clean, analyze, and visualize large datasets to identify trends and patterns. Expertise in criminological research methods and data analysis techniques.

@Ali's Linkedin

I am a data analyst with a passion for learning and growing. I was born and brought up in Hyderabad, Pakistan, and I have always been interested in technology and analytics. After completing my bachelor's in Criminology, I did a research-based internship at the Sindh Police Department. I also worked for 2 years as a security supervisor and customer service representative for a local clothing brand. During all these years, I also worked as a part-time academic tutor. I have always been analytical, and I am always looking for new ways to learn and grow. That's why I was drawn to data analytics. I attended an intensive data analytics boot camp taught by Mr. Alex Freberg, and I also completed the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate. I am now eager to start my career in data analytics. I have a strong foundation in SQL, Python, Power BI, and Tableau, and I am excited to use my skills to solve real-world problems. I am a hard worker and a team player, and I am always willing to go the extra mile. I am also a quick learner and I am always eager to take on new challenges.

I am looking for an entry-level position in data analytics where I can learn and grow, and I am confident that I would be a valuable asset to any team. Thank you!
